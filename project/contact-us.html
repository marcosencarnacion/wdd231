Lesson Specializations

Classical Violin

Classical Violin Training

Curriculum includes:

  • Mastery of standard classical repertoire
  • Proper bowing techniques and posture
  • Sight-reading and music theory
  • Preparation for auditions and competitions

Recommended for: Students interested in orchestral playing or pursuing music degrees

Jazz Violin

Jazz Violin Training

Curriculum includes:

  • Improvisation techniques
  • Jazz harmony and theory
  • Swing rhythm and syncopation
  • Playing with rhythm sections

Recommended for: Students interested in jazz ensembles or fusion styles

Covers Violin

Covers Violin Training

Curriculum includes:

  • Learning popular songs and melodies
  • Playing by ear
  • Adapting songs for violin
  • Basic recording techniques

Recommended for: Students who want to play contemporary music or perform covers

Performing Violin

Performing Violin Training

Curriculum includes:

  • Stage presence and performance techniques
  • Overcoming performance anxiety
  • Building a repertoire
  • Microphone and amplification techniques

Recommended for: Students interested in live performances or busking
